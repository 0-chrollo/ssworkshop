About Us

Bloemfontein's engine specialist that

keeps you on the road.

SS Workshop & Fitment Centre is a professional automotive workshop and parts supplier based in Bloemfontein, built on one simple belief: quality work, guaranteed results. We specialise in engine rebuilds, gearbox repairs, and full vehicle servicing for bakkie and car owners who need a team they can trust.

From minor services to complete engine replacements, we use advanced diagnostic equipment and offer a wide range of new and reconditioned engines — all with warranty. Whether you're buying parts or bringing your vehicle in, you'll always get expert advice, honest pricing, and workmanship that lasts. That's our promise.
