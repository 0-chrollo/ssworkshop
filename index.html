About Us

Cape Town's auto repair shop that

The Auto Vault is a professional automotive service and repair centre based in Cape Town, built on one simple belief: quality work, zero shortcuts. We specialise in vehicle diagnostics, auto electrical repairs, and general maintenance for car owners who deserve straight answers and real solutions.

Using modern diagnostic tools and skilled technicians, we get to the root of the problem fast. No guesswork, no inflated bills. Whether it's an electrical fault or a routine service, you'll always know exactly what's being done to your vehicle and why. That's our promise.
